Kevin Hart-Cameron Brink photo has fans 'so out of pocket'

Explore More

Kevin Hart’s height was a hot topic again on Twitter after a photo of the comedian with Stanford women’s basketball star Cameron Brink went viral on Sunday.

Hart, who stands at 5-foot-2, and Brink — a 6-foot-4 All-American — were pictured together while in Las Vegas, where NBA Summer League and other star-studded events are currently taking place.

And the internet is having a field day with their height difference.

Brink reposted the photo to her Instagram Story and wrote, “Y’all are so out of pocket I’m dead.”

Hart, who often pokes fun at his height in his standup comedy and on social media, has yet to address the photo.

“The height difference between Cameron Brink and Kevin Hart,” Bleacher Report’s HighlightHer tweeted, adding a pair of crying-laughing emojis.

“Cameron Brink towering over Kevin Hart is the unexpected content that I needed today,” another added.

Hart and Brink have been busy while in Las Vegas.

On Thursday, the comedian attended the First Annual Coramino Cup Bartender competition at Resorts World Las Vegas.

The event was presented by Gran Coramino Tequila, which was founded by Hart and tequila maker, Juan Domingo Beckmann.

Afterward, Hart joined rapper Ludacris on stage during his birthday celebration at Zouk Nightclub.

Brink, a 21-year-old forward, attended the Fanatics x NBPA Summer Players Party hosted by Michael Rubin at Tao Nightclub at The Venetian Resort, along with former WNBA star Liz Cambage, on Saturday.

The blonde beauty, who signed with CAA for NIL representation, looked stunning in a black-and-white mini dress.

Although Brink was eligible to enter the 2023 WNBA Draft, she chose to stay for her senior season and play at Stanford in 2023-24.

Last month, Brink earned a gold medal with the USA 3×3 team, winning the FIBA 3×3 World Cup in Vienna, Austria, where she was named MVP of the tournament.

Brink was also part of the gold medal U-17 and U-19 World Cup teams in 2018 and 2019.
