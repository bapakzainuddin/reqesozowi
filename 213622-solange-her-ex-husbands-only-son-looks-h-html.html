Solange and Her Ex-husband Daniel Smith's Only Son Looks like His Famous Mother in a New Photo

Solange Knowles' fifteen-year-old son, Daniel Julez, looked incredibly handsome in a new photo that showed his striking resemblance to his famous mother.

Solange Knowles shares one son, Daniel Julez, with her ex-husband, Daniel Smith, and from a recent picture, Julez is undoubtedly a replica of his mom.

In a recent Instagram post by Smith, he shared an adorable photo of his son that highlighted the uncanny resemblance between him and his mother.

Solange Knowles and her son, Julez Smith attending an event in May 2014. | Photo: Getty Images

Wearing a blue sweatshirt and black sweatpants paired with green sneakers, Smith posed for the photo as he sat in the driver's seat of a white car.

Smith shared the photo alongside a sweet caption where he sweetly expressed love for his son. He wrote;

"You come so far and still have so far to go. I'm here for the ride love u k, id.????❤️??"

Smith's post garnered reactions from fans and followers who took to his comment section to leave lovely responses to the picture.

One user wrote, "How'd he get this big so fast" while another added, "I remember attending his first few birthday parties now he got more facial hair than me." Several other followers gushed over how cute he looked.

Knowles tied the knot with Daniel in 2004 when she was just 17, and by 2007, the couple's marriage hit the rocks, and they divorced.

In 2014, Knowles opened up about her marriage to Daniel and how getting a divorce early gave her a new perspective on life. While speaking, she reportedly revealed that she and Daniel maintained a great relationship as well as a good co-parenting strategy.

Shortly after the end of Knowles' first marriage, she found love again with Alan Ferguson. After dating for a while, the couple tied the knot in an elegant New Orleans wedding, but barely five years after, the marriage came to an end.

In 2014, Solange made headlines after a security video showed her brawling with her sister's husband, Jay-Z, in an elevator at The Standard Hotel in New York City.

However, despite all of the buzzing speculations on social media, all of the parties involved didn't reveal details of the brawl.

Born on June 24, 1986, to Tina Knowles and Mathew Knowles, Solange is famously known as the younger sister of the music icon, Beyonce Knowles.

Just like her big sis, Solange has a knack for entertainment and started off as a backup dancer for Beyonce's former group, "Destiny's Child."

Following the split of the girl group, Solange went on to start her music career and has written music for stars such as Kelly Rowland, Michelle Williams, and Beyonce.
