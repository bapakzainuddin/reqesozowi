Matt Lauer net worth, age, wiki, family, biography and latest updates

The net worth, age, wiki, family, and biography of Matt Lauer are all worth discussing. Lauer is a well-known American television host, journalist, and public speaker, best known for hosting The Today Show. Despite his success, he has faced sexual allegations, leading to his resignation from several media outlets. Born on December 30, 1957, Matthew Todd Lauer, professionally known as Matt Lauer, co-hosted NBC’s Today show and contributed to Dateline NBC from 1997 to 2017. NBC terminated his contract in November 2017 due to allegations of inappropriate sexual behavior.

Lauer’s estimated net worth as of 2022 is over $70 million, earned from hosting various shows throughout his career. He has been active in the industry since 1979, hosting and producing television shows.

In terms of career, Lauer began as a producer before becoming an on-air presenter.
