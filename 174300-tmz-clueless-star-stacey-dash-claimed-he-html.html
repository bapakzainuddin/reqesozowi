'Clueless' Star Stacey Dash Claimed Her Husband and His 3 Kids Conspired to Get Her Arrested in 911

Stacey Dash claims her husband and her three stepchildren conspired to have her arrested for domestic violence.

On Sunday, September 29, 2019, 911 received a distress call from a woman claiming she was being mistreated by her husband.

The actress and the lawyer had barely known each other for 10 days when they tied the knot

STACY DASH CLAIMS HUSBAND AND STEPCHILDREN CONSPIRED AGAINST HER

Dash's husband, attorney Jeffrey Marty showed the police scratches on his arms he alleged were made by his wife, and his three children - aged 15, 14 and 10 - corroborated his story.

THE INCIDENT THAT LED TO STACY DASH'S ARREST

According to Dash, the incident occurred when she was trying to get her 15-year-old stepdaughter to do her homework.

“We were all arguing, I asked his daughter to get out of my face, she was in my face. I pushed her back and he put me into a chokehold,” she said.

STACY DASH CLAIMS JEFFERY MARTY'S INJURIES ARE SELF INFLICTED

“They’re just talking with each other about how to put me in jail. He’s taking pictures of scratches on his arm that I did not put there. But I have red marks on my neck (...) They’re just talking with each other about how to put me in jail,” Dash told the dispatcher.

STACY DASH'S HUSBAND BAILED HER OUT

Dash was taken to Land O’ Lakes detention center and later released on a $500 bond when her husband bailed her out.

Marty is Dash's fourth husband. The actress was previously married three times: to producer Brian Lovell, British executive James Maby, and actor Emmanuel Xeureb.

Dash has two children: son Austin, 28, from her relationship with singer Christopher Williams; and Lola, 16, whom she shares with ex-husband Brian Lovell.
