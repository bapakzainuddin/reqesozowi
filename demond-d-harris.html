Demond D. Harris Metropolitan Funeral

God Called Demond D.Harris Home On October 17th 2021 he was born March 9th,1984 in Norfolk Virginia.  He attended Norview High School In Norfolk Virginia & Votech Where he took courses in Carpentry, “Demond had a passion for building”. He was preceded  by his Mother Lila Harris 2010. He also left to cherish the memory of his brother Devon Harris Sr. of Norfolk , Virginia; A niece (A’lila Harris), (nephew Devon Harris Jr.) two Uncles Rev. Ralph Harris (Brenda) of Rocky Mount, Virginia and Thaddeus Harris (Roxanne) of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania; And also four first cousins Tiffany, Shannon, Antoine, And Shera, and a host of family and friends. You Will Truly Be Missed Demond (BigBoy) Harris, but never forgotten.
