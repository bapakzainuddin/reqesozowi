Who is Ronnie Wood? 

Today we are going to talk about rock guitarist with the classic rock band Rolling Stones, and Small Faces Ronnie Wood. Wood also has collaborated with Jeff Beck. Wood is an English musician, guitarist, bassist, songwriter, and artist. 

Ronnie Wood was born as the eldest of three children in a family of English water gypsies or bargees on June 1, 1947, in Hillingdon, England. According to him, his generation members were the first in the family born on drylands.

He grew up in Yiewsley and studied at St. Stephen's Infant School. Then, in St. The England Primary School Church of Matthew and  St Martin's C of E Secondary Modern School. He subsequently attended Ealing Art College in West London. His two older brothers were musicians and graphic artists, Art Wood, and Ted Wood. Wood is yet to provide details about his parents. 

During his teens in 1964, he started his musical career as a guitarist with a group called ' The Birds. ' He wrote or co-wrote many of the group's recorded songs. 

After disbanding ' The Birds ' in 1967, he entered ' Jeff Beck Group ' as a bass player. There were released two band albums, ' Truth ' and ' Beck-Ola, which met with restricted achievement. 

He also performed with the mod group ' Creation ' for a short time. Not to forget, he is an accomplished painter whose paintings found a place at London's Drury Lane Theatre.
