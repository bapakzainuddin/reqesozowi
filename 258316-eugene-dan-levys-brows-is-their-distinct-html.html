Eugene and Dan Levy's Brows Is Their Distinctive Feature inside Their Best Family Photos

The co-creators of the Netflix hit television sitcom "Schitt's Creek," Eugene and Dan Levy, lived a plainly ordinary life together before Dan started acting professionally.

Speaking of his typical childhood in Toronto, Dan revealed to People recently that he got into trouble a lot growing up with his legendary comedic actor father Eugene.

Dan added that Eugene made him complete his homework and grounded him. Meanwhile, scroll down below to see some of the best family photos of Eugene and Dan Levy.

Eugene Levy and Dan Levy at The Shrine Auditorium on January 19, 2020 in Los Angeles, California. | Photo: Getty Images

FAMOUS BUSHY BROWS

Eugene passed down not just his acting talents but his bushy eyebrows, which were just starting to develop in the adorable throwback picture shared by Dan on Instagram in June 2020.

BORN TO BE A STAR

While Eugene never intended for his children to feel pressured into becoming actors, the inevitable occurred. Would you like more proof that Dan was born to be a star?

In February 2018, Dan, who began his career as a television host, took to Instagram to share a theatrical throwback of himself, captioning the post with "Me. Being dramatic (for a change). #FBF"

THEY ARE DEFINITELY TWINNING

In June 2017, Dan posted a precious picture of himself and his father posing with the same facial expression. In the caption, the star wrote that they have been working on their act since 1983.

Although Dan liked to mimic his father as a kid, his hair was a big no-no for him as an adult. Meanwhile, Dan credited his iconic father for helping him learn everything he knows.

YEARBOOK PHOTOGRAPH

In October 2017, Dan shared a photo of himself wearing his black-framed glasses in a ninth-grade yearbook photo. He looks nearly identical to his dad in a picture that he posted in December 2016.

STRIKING RESEMBLANCE

In January 2019, Dan's pic with his father received numerous comments that they look almost identical, which is crazy. On captioning the photo, Dan quipped that their pose took them an hour to figure out.
