Monique Montgomery, The University of Southern Mississippi alumnus, passes away suddenly

Columbus, MS – The unexpected passing of Monique Montgomery has left a profound impact on all those who knew her. On Sunday, October 29, 2023, Monique Montgomery was confirmed to have passed away, leaving behind a community deeply saddened by the news of her untimely departure. While the specific circumstances surrounding her death remain undisclosed, the public has rallied together to express their condolences and support for her grieving family during this difficult time.

Pauline Rogers posted on Facebook,

“Beloved Monique, my sister & friend. I did not know that October 11th at 8:57am would be the last. You ran for a Judge seat on this side but did not obtain it because the LORD had prepared the highest judge seat for you where the saints shall judge the world. Judge the world Judge Montgomery. I rest my case. May you rest in peace.”

In response to the outpouring of sympathy, numerous individuals have come forward to pay tribute to Monique Montgomery, sharing heartfelt messages and memories of the moments they shared with her. As the family copes with the loss, it is encouraged that the community keep them in their thoughts and prayers, providing solace and strength throughout their grieving process.
