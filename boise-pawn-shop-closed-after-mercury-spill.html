Boise pawn shop closed after mercury spill

BOISE, Idaho (CBS 2) — A Boise area pawn shop is closed for the day after a mercury spill.

Boise Fire says crews were sent out to the First Nation Pawn at 919 N. Orchard Street.

If anyone was at the pawn shop at about 10:30 a.m. Monday morning you're asked to call 208-377-6790 so crews can determine if you were exposed to mercury.
