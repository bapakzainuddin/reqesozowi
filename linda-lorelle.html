The former CBS, NBC, journalist Linda Lorelle is married to her husband Lou Gregory since 1990.

Linda Lorelle is the prestigious award-winning media personality who currently hosts the podcast Our Voices Matter with Linda Lorelle. Lorelle is also well-identified as the founder of the non-profit making organization Linda Lorelle Scholarship Fund Inc.

Linda was born on August 11, in Chicago, Illinois, USA, to Clay Lorelle (father) and Anita Clarice Lorelle (mother). Besides, nothing much is known about her early life details.

Academic Details

Regarding education, Linda attended Faulkner School for Girls. Later, she earned her B.A degree in Psychology and B.A in the Italian language from Stanford University. Afterward, Lorelle joined the University of Missouri-Columbia and graduated with a Master's of Arts degree in Broadcast Journalism.

Who Is Linda Lorelle's Husband?

The media personality Linda is in a marital relationship with Lou Gregory. Linda's husband Lou is also the founder and co-chairman of the Linda Lorelle Scholarship Fund. The organization provides college scholarships, support, and guidance to Houston area high school students who are in need of financial assistance.

How and when Lou and Linda met it is still unclear, but it seems like the duo dated several months before they exchanged their wedding vows in June 1990.

As of now, Mr. and Mrs. Gregory are sharing a good bond as a perfect married pair. They are celebrating their 30-years of marriage anniversary merrily without involving in any controversies related to their separation.

From the strong marital bond, Linda and her husband Lou shared a beautiful daughter Lindsey Gregory in May 1994. Their only kid Lindsey who studied at Texas Christian University works as a business system analyst at the government services company Perspecta.

Professional Career

Linda began her professional journalism career by working as a reporter for KMOV-TV (CBS affiliated). She served there for 2-years 2 months from August 1987 to September 1989. Next, in September 1989, she joined KPRC-TV (NBC affiliated) as a news anchor and worked there till February 2006.

Then from May 2006 to February 2007, Lorelle worked as a contributing correspondent for KRIV-TV (Fox 26). Similarly, from May 2017 to March 2018, Linda worked for Pink Petro TV as a co-anchor.

Likewise, she is serving as a principal at Outreach Strategists LLC since June 2014. Moreover, Lorelle is also the executive producer and CEO of Lorelle Media.

Net Worth

Linda has an estimated net worth of around $1 million which she accumulated from her various professional works. For the information, another journalist Joelle Garguilo enjoys a net value of around $600 thousand. 

Being a journalist Lorelle might have earned an impressive sum of wealth. Lorelle is also an entrepreneur. From there to she is earning a good sum of wealth. Not only that, but she is also a philanthropist who runs a non-profit making organization called the Linda Lorelle Scholarship Fund. 

Their organization whose main mission is to provide college scholarship, support for the high school students has awarded more than $4 million to deserving students. 

Awards and Accolades

As mentioned above, Lorelle has received various prestigious awards. She is awarded the 2005 AWRT Gracie Award, 2005 Houston Press Club Award, 2004 Emmy Award, 2000 Texas Associated Press Award, and 2006 Loving Hearts Caring Hands Award, among others.
