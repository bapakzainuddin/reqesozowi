Human remains in San Fernando Valley identified as missing Guatemalan man; suspect arrested

Human remains in San Fernando: A man has been arrested in North Hills on suspicion of killing a Guatemalan man who had been missing since June, after human remains were discovered at his residence. The remains were found under a structure that had been removed, and it is unclear what led authorities to make the discovery. The victim has been identified as Carlos DeLeon, and the suspect, Russell Robinson, is being held without bail. The motive for the killing and the relationship between the two individuals are currently unknown. If you have any information on this case, please contact the Los Angeles Police.
