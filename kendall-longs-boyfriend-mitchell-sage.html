Meet Bachelor in Paradises Kendall Longs new boyfriend after split with Joe Amabile

Source: IG

  • Kendall Long is a reality tv star and a creative director
  • She competed on the 22nd season of The Bachelor
  • Again, she returned for the 5th and 6th seasons of Bachelor in Paradise
  • During her time on the reality show, she dated fellow contestant Joe Amabile however, their relationship didn’t last forever
  • She is now official with her new boyfriend Mitchell Saga

The Bachelor star, Kendall Long has gone Instagram official with a new boyfriend. The reality star’s new romantic relationship announcement comes less than a year after her breakup with Joe Amabile.

Long shared a series of pictures taken alongside her new boyfriend Mitchell Saga on Saturday, April 9. She also captioned the pictures,

“By the way, I’m in love”

She then added,

“And just like our relationship, these pictures get progressively weirder”

As soon as she dropped the cheerful pictures, several celebrities and well-wishers showered their love on the new couple. Her comment section is flooded with a congratulatory messages.

The Bachelor season 21 contestant Astrid Loch wrote,

“Ummmm cuties,”

Similarly, actress Syd Wilder commented,

“Ahhh my heart is so happy for you!! You deserve all the love in the world!! Congratulations!!”

Further, her one follower noted that she is also dating an aerospace engineer, and “they are the best”. The lovebirds appeared cozy in several photos which can be seen taken at a local zoo, during a picnic in a park, and embracing one another during date nights.

Who is her new boyfriend?

The name of her new boyfriend is Mitchell Saga. While uploading their official pictures the 30-year-old author of Just Curious: A Notebook of Questions also tagged her boyfriend on her social media.

According to his Instagram handle, Mitchell is an aerospace engineer who describes himself as a “Half aerospace engineer, half athlete, half matured adult.” However, his Instagram account is private at the moment.

Kendall Long’s past love affairs

Prior to Mitchell, Kendall has been in a much-publicized relationship with fellow contestant Joe Amabile. The two met each other during Season 5 of Bachelor in Paradise and eventually started romance in 2018.

However, their relationship wasn’t fully stable as they have an on-again and off-again relationship. Later, everything seemed right between them, and also moved in together in Los Angeles in April 2019.

The duo even competes on Dancing With the Stars together. Nonetheless, the flame didn’t last forever and they ultimately called it quits on their relationship in January 2020. Then, they released a joint statement mentioning that their separation was mutual.

After that, she was rumored to be dating Bachelor in Paradise alum Blake Horstmann in August 2021. However, the speculations were not true.
