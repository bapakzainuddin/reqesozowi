Do You Know America's Reality TV star Shamicka Gibbs Is Battling Lupus? Know More Facts About Her

Do you about Shamicka Gibbs's current relationship status? The former reality TV star made her name in the showbiz industry earlier as the wife of Bad Boys star Martin Lawrence.

Shamicka suffered from Lupus 

The talented businessperson Shamicka is battling a life-threatening disease called Lupus. Lupus is an auto-immune disease that takes the life of the victim if not controlled early.

With her stress-free lifestyle and balanced diet, Gibbs manages to control her diseases. Her 167,000 followers on Instagram and 22,000 on Twitter wish and pray for her speedy recovery.
