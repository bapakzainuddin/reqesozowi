KUWTK Star Khlo Kardashian Channels Anna Nicole Smith's Iconic Look in New Photo Shoot

Khloe Kardashian channels Anna Nicole Smith's iconic Guess campaign pose. Her family and even ex-boyfriend comment on her new look.

Wearing an all denim outfit, the "Revenge Body with Khloe" star, Khloe Kardashian recreates icon, Anna Nicole Smith poses, she captions the picture:

"Channeling Anna Nicole Smith from her Guess campaign."

The reality star nails the former Playboy and Guess model's signature pout and smouldering eyes.

Anna Nicole Smith, at an event where is was announced that she would be a Guess jeans model on February 8 1993 | Source: Getty Images

It is reported, her Instagram post received close to one million likes, within the first three hours. Getting in on the action, Khloe's ex-boyfriend, Tristan Thompson, also commented on the picture.

According to E! News, Tristan commented simply saying, "Perfection." This was followed by a heart-eyes emoji. The comment has since been deleted.

A source as revealed to PEOPLE, despite their break-up, after his infidelity was made public, Khloe and Tristan are on good terms. They are working on co-parenting for the sake of their daughter True Thompson. The source explains how they have manged to get along, by adding:

"Tristan wants to be a good, present dad to True. He's committed to raising her in a loving environment and also wants to be in a good place with Khloé."

Khloe isn't the only Kardashian, who is a fan of Anna Nicole Smith, Kim Kardashian admits in her comment that the former model is "her fav." Khloe's family absolutely love the 90’s inspired look.
