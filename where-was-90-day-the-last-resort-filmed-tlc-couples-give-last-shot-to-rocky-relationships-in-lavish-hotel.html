Where was '90 Day: The Last Resort' filmed? TLC couples give last shot to rocky relationships in lav

THE KEYS, FLORIDA: '90 Day: The Last Resort' is all set to provide a last chance to previous couples from the '90 Day Fiancé' universe to retrieve their respective relationships.

In the TLC show spinoff, five couples will seek therapy and clear the miscommunication as they find out if they can heal old wounds or not.

The TLC show stars will participate in a couples retreat in a resort as they navigate several issues regarding anger, trust, jealousy, intimacy, and sex. The upcoming spin-off also features multiple group therapies, past life regressions, intense couples' sessions, and on-and-off-resort activities.

Where was '90 Day: The Last Resort' filmed?

'90 Day: The Last Resort' couples jetted off to the exotic and lavish resort in Florida as they try to mend their relationships. Most of the episodes of the TLC show spin-off have been filmed in the Florida Keys, a coral cay archipelago situated off the southern coast of Florida.

'90 Day: The Last Resort' will take the viewers in and around the Isla Bella Beach Resort in the city of Marathon, located right in the middle of the Florida Keys. TLC has recently released stunning photos of the luxurious resort.

However, the cast and crew members of the TLC show were spotted at various spots across the resort, including the bar, swimming pools, and the beach.

Isla Bella Beach Resort has been stretched over a mile of waterfront property and is home to 24 beautiful oceanfront acres with vibrant blue sea and exquisite white sand in front, a beautiful beach bar, five swimming pools, and access to several adventurous activities, including fishing.

What's new about TLC's spinoff '90 Day: The Last Resort'?

In '90 Day: The Last Resort', five couples give their last shot to their relationship as they reached a breaking point. Angela Deem and Michael Ilesanmi; Yara Zaya and Jovi Dufren; Asuelu Pulaa and Kalani Faagata; Molly Hopkins and Kelly Brown; Big Ed and Liz Woods star in the upcoming spin-off.

The couples will try to repair their relationships in a last-ditch effort before they decide whether they want to stay together or split in the last episode.

Therapists Petey Silveira, Dr Janie Lacy, and Dr Jason Prendergast will join the couples in the resort while fate will decide the future of the couples' rocky relationships.

'90 Day: The Last Resort' premieres on Monday, August 14 at 9 pm on TLC.
